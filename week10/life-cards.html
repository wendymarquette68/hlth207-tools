Printable Life Cards — You Just Turned 65

Print this page and cut along the card edges. Distribute one card per student (or per pair). For 25 students, print 4 copies and cut — you will have enough for everyone with some extras.

How to Use These Cards — You Just Turned 65

1
Hand one card to each student (or pair). Students with the same card will have identical situations — that's fine.
2
Students select their matching card letter in the Student App on their phone or laptop.
3
The instructor reveals scenarios one at a time on the projector. Students make decisions in their app. After each scenario, class discusses briefly.
4
At the end, students compare results: Who ran out of savings? Who qualified for Medicaid? Who still had options in Scenario 6?
