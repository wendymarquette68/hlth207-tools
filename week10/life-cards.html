Printable Life Cards — You Just Turned 65

Print this page and cut along the card edges. Distribute one card per student or per pair. For a class of 25, print 4 copies.

How to Use These Cards

1
Hand one card to each student or pair. Students with the same card letter will have identical situations — that is fine.
2
Students select their matching card letter in the Student App on their phone or laptop.
3
The instructor reveals scenarios one at a time on the projector. Students make decisions in their app after each scenario.
4
At the end, compare results: Who ran out of savings? Who qualified for Medicaid? Who still had options in Scenario 6?
